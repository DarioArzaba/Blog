* American illustrator of children's books (1863 - 1935).
* Despite never having children, she is considered one of the best cartoonist in children's literature.
* She studied pedagogy but as soon as she finished her studies she dedicated herself to illustration 
* Since she was passionate about drawing she started her artistic studies at the Pennsylvania Academy of the Fine Arts.
* In 1889 she found work as an illustrator for "Ladies Home Journal" and later for "Good Housekeeping". 
* Once she gained financial stability, Jessie moved in with two other illustrators earning the nickname "The Red Rose Girls".
