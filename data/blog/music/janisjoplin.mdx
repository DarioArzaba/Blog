American rock singer (Psychedelic and Soul) started in 1969.

- Janis Joplin (Vocalist and songwriter)

* Often called the *Queen of Psychedelic Rock*. She was born in Texas in 1943 along with two minor brothers. 
* A friend of her presents her to Bessie Smith, Ma. Rainey and Lead Belly who inspire her to be a singer.
* She is inspired by Odetta, Billie Holiday and Big Mama Thornton. Her first demo was *The Typewriter Tape* with seven songs. 
* She did cocaine, heroin, many psychedelic drugs and alcohol. Her favorite drink was *Southern Comfort* a whiskey liquor.
* In 1965 due to the effects of injected methamphetamine she returns to Texas and joins *Big Brother and The Holding Company*. 
* Her fame in hippie community induces her consume more drugs. She leaves the band and forms a new one called *Kosmic Blues Band*.
* By 1969 she was spending more than 3,000 dollars on heroin. She appeared at Woodstock Festival and then at Madison Square Garden.
* After a trip to Brazil to get clean she forms *Full Tilt Boogie Band* but dies soon after from heroin overdose.
* Her famous psychedelic Porsche was sold at 2 million dollars in 2009.
