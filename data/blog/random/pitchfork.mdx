American music magazine founded by Ryan Schreiber in 1995. The site publishes information and album reviews, focused on the indie genre. PitchFork continues to sell a physical version of their magazine titled *The Pitch Fork Review*. They have a list of featured songs and albums by year, articles, reviews and photos. They also have a list ranking artists and a music videos section called *PitchForkTV*.
